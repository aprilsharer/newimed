Creamy Cajun Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Bag of Mini Sweet Peppers (chopped)
  • 1lb of Chicken Tenders (uncooked)
  • 12oz cream cheese spread
  • 1tbs cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400.
  2. Line a greased 9x13 with the chopped peppers.
  3. Place Chicken on top of pepper layer.
  4. Generously sprinkle the chicken tenders with cajun seasoning.
  5. Top with small scoops of cream cheese on each chicken tender.
  6. Sprinkle cheddar cheese on top of the tenders last.
  7. Bake uncovered for 25 minutes, or until chicken is cooked fully.

Everything Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 uncooked Salmon Fillets
  • 8oz cream cheese spread
  • 1tbs everything but the bagel seasoning

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425.
  2. Place salmon fillets on a parchment lined pan.
  3. Top each fillet with cream cheese spread in an even layer.
  4. Sprinkle each fillet with everything but the bagel seasoning.
  5. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes, or until salmon is cooked fully.
  6. Serve with baked potato, grilled veggies, or rice.
  7. Enjoy!

Burito Bowls

Ingredients:

  • 1lb of Chicken Tenders (uncooked)
  • 8oz of store bought queso of choice
  • 1 bag of Birds Eye Power Blend (Southwest Style)
  • Diced avocado (optional)
  • Shredded Mexican Cheese (optional)
  • Pico de gallo (optional)

Directions:

  1. Place chicken tenders in a crockpot with 1 cup chicken broth, cook on low for 6 hours.
  2. Shred chicken in the crockpot, soak in remaining broth for 5 minutes, then drain excess broth.
  3. Add queso to the shredded chicken in the crockpot, and set to high for 5 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, prepare the power blend veggie rice mix according to package instructions.
  5. Layer each bowl with rice mix, queso chicken mix, and top with cheese, pico de gallo, and avocado.

Southwest Monterays

Ingredients:

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 taco seasoning packet
  • 1 package of tostadas
  • Topping ideas: shredded lettuce, tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream

Directions:

  1. Cook ground beef and drain.
  2. Mix in refried beans, taco seasoning, and enchilada sauce in a pan on medium heat for 3 minutes.
  3. Heat tostadas according to package instructions.
  4. Top heated tostadas with meat/bean mixture, shredded cheese, lettuce, and diced tomatoes.
  5. Enjoy!

Mickey Mouse Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbs white sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbs oil
  • 1 egg
  • Chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Combine dry ingredients and stir well.
  2. Add oil, milk, and egg, mix until smooth.
  3. Heat skillet with 1 tbs of butter or oil on medium-high heat.
  4. Pour one large circle in the center of the pan, followed by two smaller circles for ears.
  5. Place chocolate chips in a smiley face on the batter before flipping.
  6. Flip pancake and cook until both sides are golden.

Chinese Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of shredded cabbage or broccoli slaw
  • 1 bag of wonton strips
  • 1 rotisserie chicken, deboned and shredded
  • Sesame Ginger Dressing




Directions:

  1. Combine chicken and cabbage in a large salad bowl.
  2. Toss in desired amount of Sesame Ginger Dressing.
  3. Top each bowl of salad generously with wonton strips.
  4. Enjoy!

5 Ingredient Cajun Chicken

This is my go-to rainy day activty! All you need for this one is a spin-mop bucket, cocoa powder, 9x13 pan, and toy cars. To prep the activity just sprinkle cocoa powder on the bottom of your pan and stir in a tablespoon of water at a time to make this taste-safe mud your desired consistency. Then, fill your spin-mop bucket a quarter full of water and you're good to go. For this activity kids will practice fine motor skills and problem solving by creating this open-ended muddy carwash for their toy cars. My boys race their cars in "mud" and then pass the car off to the "car washer" who dunks the dirty car in the mop bucket, and then spins it dry!

Southwest Monterays

When was the last day that you didn’t make a single purchase? Having online shopping at our fingertips makes it tempting to buy something as soon as we think of it. What if you just left it in your cart and re-evaluated at the end of the week? Start off with one day a week that you won’t need to make any essential purchases and go the whole day without spending. Challenge yourself to increase your number of “no-spend” days per month.
